About me

My name is Xia Zhengmin. I got my Ph.D. degree from the department of electronic engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2012. My research interests are Network traffic management and abnormal detection. I have published several papers in these areas. I also intrested in some other areas about information security such as firewall, VPN, cryptography, and Trusted Computing.

Projects

National Defense Basic Pre-Study Project. Major Program of National 863. Information system rank protection evaluation. National Key Research and Development Project.
